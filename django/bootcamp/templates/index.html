WHO WE ARE

Court Science is a federally incorporated not-for-profit organization that aims to bridge the gap between STEM and sports by making the power of Data Science accessible to youth and sports enthusiasts worldwide, through our free online interactive data visualization platform and community initiatives. In light of COVID-19, we have shifted all of our outreach efforts online including a free Sports Science Camp with a series of live virtual international workshops geared towards youth interested in exploring their favourite sport through the lens of STEM and creative problem solving, while learning about Data Science.

COURT SCIENCE VIRTUAL CAMP

Court Science Virtual Camp is a first of its kind interactive educational program where sports meets active learning, putting math and science in an exciting new light!

Virtual Learning

Regular workshops offered free of cost, aimed at K-12 students, bring the virtual classroom to the court

Expert Instructors

A curriculum developed and taught by knowledgeable and passionate instructors

Engagement

Students investigate questions about sports through data exploration using the scientific method and creative problem solving, before presenting their findings and engaging in friendly discussions with other campers
